Abacha - African Salad

About The Dish

African Salad, (locally known as Abacha) is one of the meals that is mostly and enjoyed by the Ibo Speaking people of Nigeria. Its native taste is so distinctive, making it a delicacy to be enjoyed by all. It is usually served as a side dish in special events such as traditional weddings, burial ceremonies, annivesaries or the visit of a special guest. It is best served with Palm Wine, a local drink tapped from special palm trees, but you can always enjoy it with any chilled drink of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 300g of Abacha (dried shredded cassava)
  • 100g of Ugba (fermented African Oilbean seeds)
  • 200ml of Palm Oil
  • 2 tablespoonful of pounded crayfish
  • 1 large (or two small) Onions
  • Red Chlli pepper
  • Salt
  • 100g of finely chopped fresh Garde Egg leaves
  • Boiled Dried Fsh or Stockfish
